Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. What should I do if the app crashes or fails to launch?

  • Please ensure your device’s operating system is up to date.
  • Verify that you have installed the latest version of Tap Master Pro.
  • If the problem persists, try uninstalling and then reinstalling the app.

Q2. My score or combo isn’t updating correctly. What can I do?

  • This may be caused by temporary glitches or cache issues. Try restarting the app.
  • Make sure you are using the latest version, as updates may include bug fixes.

Q3. What should I do if tap inputs seem unresponsive or delayed?

  • Touch responsiveness can vary by device. First, check if other apps are experiencing similar issues.
  • If the problem continues, please provide detailed information (device model, OS version, and the circumstances under which the issue occurs) when contacting support.

Q4. Which devices are supported by Tap Master Pro?

  • Tap Master Pro is compatible with iPhones and iPads running iOS 13 or later.
  • For more detailed device requirements, please check the app’s listing on the App Store.

Q5. How can I provide feedback or get further assistance?

  • You can reach us by email (see below) or via the contact form available on this page.
  • We welcome your feedback and use it to help improve Tap Master Pro in future updates.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Restart the App: Sometimes closing and reopening the app can resolve temporary issues.
  • Clear Cache: If problems persist, try clearing your device’s cache.
